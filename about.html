About Us

Northgate Building Group is a premium residential construction company delivering architecturally designed homes and renovations across Sydney, Newcastle and the Hunter Region.

Led by Jordan O'Driscoll and Chris Harrison, Northgate combines high level technical expertise with genuine care and integrity.

Together they bring almost four decades of experience across bespoke homes, boutique developments and complex architectural builds, managed with the systems of a larger contractor and the personal touch of a family business.

Every Northgate project is driven by one goal: to make building feel clear, calm and collaborative.

Why Us

Northgate was started to bring back Integrity, Clarity, and Genuine care to residential construction.

Too often, homeowners are left feeling uncertain, misinformed, or disconnected from the people actually building their home. We believe the process should feel different.

At Northgate, we exist to make building simple, honest, and enjoyable.

We take the time to understand what matters most, communicate with transparency, and build with craftsmanship that lasts.

Because for us, it is not just about delivering a beautiful home, it is about giving our clients clarity in every decision, confidence in every stage, and care in every detail.
